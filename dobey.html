Dobby

Dobby is a house-elf whose defining traits are loyalty, innocence, courage, and an intense desire for freedom and dignity. Initially introduced as a timid and self-punishing servant bound by cruel magical laws, he defies expectations by secretly helping Harry Potter despite the risk of severe punishment, driven by a pure moral instinct rather than obligation.
After gaining his freedom, Dobby becomes fiercely independent yet remains devoted to helping others, choosing to work at Hogwarts for fair conditions and wages, which reflects his belief in self-worth and equality.
